Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm [Myles Isaac]

For over [4] years, I’ve wandered forests, deserts, and oceans to capture the untamed beauty of wildlife. My journey began when [ "I encountered a lion in Rongai"], and since then, I’ve dedicated my lens to telling the stories of creatures often unseen.

Through my work, I aim to "bridge the gap between humans and the natural world". Every image is a testament to resilience, fragility, and the raw poetry of nature.

My Philosophy

"Wildlife isn’t just subject matter—it’s a dialogue. I wait for hours, sometimes days, to capture a single moment of truth: a tiger’s gaze, an elephant’s quiet sorrow, or the fleeting grace of a bird in flight."
